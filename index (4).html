🌱 Soil Health & Composting

Understanding your soil is the foundation of profitable sustainable farming.

What is Soil Health Index (SHI)?
SHI measures organic matter, drainage, and microbial activity. A score above 70 means your soil can support high-yield crops without synthetic fertilizer dependency.


How to improve SHI:
• Add compost from kitchen/crop waste (+5 SHI)
• Practice crop rotation — grow legumes to fix nitrogen (+8 SHI)
• Avoid over-tilling — damages soil structure (-3 SHI)
• Mulch between rows to retain moisture (+3 SHI)
